Reclaim

Sustainable + Secondhand Maternity Baby Kids Clothing

About

Reclaim is an inclusively sustainable clothing store for pregnant people and little ones from birth to 5 years. We stock new and used maternity, baby and kids clothing that has been purchased from customers like you.

We stock new Canadian brands that focus on environmentally conscious practices and future friendly textiles. In a world of fast fashion and ever changing trends, it can be incredibly difficult to get the look you want without sacrificing something - that is where Reclaim comes in!

Our pieces are carefully selected and curated to keep up with current styles while maintaining accessibility through sustainable practices, low cost to consumer, and our ever changing inventory thanks to our fashion forward customers!
